About Me

Engineering that
respects the planet

I'm a Mechanical Engineer from ENIM (National Engineering School of Monastir) working at VALEO as a Method Engineer in plastic injection manufacturing. My work lives at the intersection of process engineering and sustainability.

From validating recycled material ratios for injection molding, to redesigning changeover systems with SMED and lean tools — I believe every engineering decision is also an environmental one.

Outside the factory floor, I'm driven by circular economy thinking, hands-on R&D, and finding smarter ways to do more with less.
